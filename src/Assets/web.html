Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to download software or is it web-based?

Bearest is available as a web app accessible from any browser. We also offer a mobile app for (Android/iOS/both) for on-the-go access to your tasks.

What makes Bearest different from other productivity apps?

Bearest offers a unique blend of task management, time tracking, and goal setting, all in one intuitive interface.

Does the app integrate with other tools I use?

Yes, Bearest integrates with popular calendar, email, and project management tools to streamline your workflow.

Is my data secure with Bearest?

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted and protected with the highest security standards.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?

You can continue to access and use your data after canceling your subscription. However, you may lose access to certain features.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your Bearest plan whenever you need to adjust your subscription.
